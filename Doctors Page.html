Welcome to Our Doctors Page

At Lumen Care, we believe that exceptional healthcare begins with an exceptional team of medical professionals. Our doctors are at the heart of our commitment to providing the highest quality of care to our patients. With a diverse range of specialties, years of experience, and a passion for patient-centered care, our team of doctors is dedicated to ensuring your well-being and health.
On this page, you will find detailed profiles of our esteemed doctors, highlighting their areas of expertise, qualifications, and personal approaches to healthcare. Whether you're looking for a general practitioner, a specialist, or a surgeon, our doctors are here to offer you the compassionate and comprehensive care you deserve.

Dr. Andrew Collins

Specialization: Cardiology
Experience: 15+ years
Education:


About :

Dr. Andrew Collins is a renowned cardiologist with over 15 years of experience in diagnosing and treating a wide range of heart-related conditions. He is known for his expertise in interventional cardiology, particularly in performing complex procedures such as angioplasties and stent placements. Dr. Collins has a strong commitment to providing personalized care and focuses on the early detection and prevention of cardiovascular diseases.

Proffesional Highlights :

  • Published numerous research articles in leading medical journals on topics related to cardiovascular health.
  • Member of the American College of Cardiology (ACC) and the European Society of Cardiology (ESC).
  • Recognized for his excellence in patient care and awarded "Top Doctor" by Health Magazine for three consecutive years.
