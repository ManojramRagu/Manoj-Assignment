Our Branches

Colombo Branch

Centrally located in the bustling city of Colombo, the Lunem Care Colombo Branch offers a wide range of medical services, from emergency care to specialized treatments. With cutting-edge technology, modern infrastructure, and a dedicated team of doctors and healthcare professionals, this branch is committed to delivering high-quality healthcare to patients from all walks of life. The Colombo Branch also features advanced diagnostic facilities, a comprehensive outpatient department, and a dedicated maternity unit, ensuring holistic care for all. With a focus on patient safety and comfort, Lunem Care Colombo is a leader in healthcare innovation, constantly adapting to meet the evolving needs of its community. It collaborates with international medical institutions for research and training, enhancing the quality of care through the latest medical practices and technologies. Additionally, it offers telemedicine services, enabling remote consultations and follow-ups for patients unable to visit in person.

Kandy Branch

Nestled in the picturesque city of Kandy, the Lunem Care Kandy Branch combines serene surroundings with top-notch medical facilities. The branch is designed to provide personalized healthcare services, including specialty clinics, diagnostic services, and wellness programs, ensuring patients receive the best care in a tranquil environment. In addition to general medicine, it offers specialized care in cardiology, orthopedics, and pediatric services. The Kandy Branch also features a rehabilitation center, catering to patients recovering from surgeries or chronic illnesses, with a multidisciplinary team dedicated to holistic healing. This branch is ideal for those seeking a peaceful yet professional healthcare experience. Its location also makes it a hub for medical tourism, attracting patients from nearby regions and abroad, providing culturally sensitive care to diverse communities, and offering special packages that combine medical treatment with the unique cultural experiences of Kandy.

Jaffna Branch

The Lunem Care Jaffna Branch, located in the culturally rich northern city of Jaffna, focuses on accessible and community-oriented healthcare. Equipped with modern amenities and a team fluent in the local languages, this branch offers comprehensive medical services, ranging from general practice to specialist consultations, catering specifically to the needs of the northern community. The Jaffna Branch emphasizes preventative healthcare, with regular health camps, screenings, and educational programs aimed at improving community health awareness. It also provides a well-equipped dialysis unit, women’s health services, and a mental health support clinic, all designed to deliver inclusive, patient-centered care in the region. Moreover, the branch works closely with local organizations to address unique health challenges faced by the community, including nutrition, maternal health, and infectious diseases, strengthening public health initiatives through collaboration and outreach programs.
