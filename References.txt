Colors for WEB:

Cyan (Primary Accent): #00B8D9
	This shade of cyan is suitable as it evokes a sense of calm and trust, which is ideal for healthcare settings. Use it sparingly for call-to-action buttons, links, or icons.

Soft White (Background): #F4F4F9
	A clean, soft white background promotes a sense of cleanliness and openness, which is crucial for a hospital website.

Light Gray (Text or Background Sections): #E0E0E0
	A light gray can be used for text, backgrounds, or section dividers, providing a neutral tone that maintains a clean and professional look.

Navy Blue (Headers or Highlights): #003366
	A deep navy blue adds a sense of professionalism and stability. It can be used for headers, menus, or important highlights to contrast with the cyan.

Soft Green (Accents or Highlights): #8BC34A
	A soft green is often associated with healing and wellness. It can be used for subtle accents or to highlight important sections like patient services or emergency contacts.
